02 — Individual Computer Setup

Per-Person Configuration

Each person on the team uses Claude on their own computer via their own account. This is the setup each individual needs — separate from what lives in Google Drive.

Claude Pro is required — $20/month per person. Without it, Claude Projects and Knowledge documents are not available, and none of the shared context system will work.
RolePlan RequiredPrimary Claude Uses
AdminProEmails, SOPs, research briefs, outreach drafts
Program ManagerProCurricula, event planning, webinar outlines, retreat logistics
Video EditorProTranscript → clip concepts, SEO metadata, chapter markers
Social MediaProCaptions, content calendars, avatar research, trend reports
JeannieProReview support, strategic planning, team comms, all of the above
JenniferProBook writing, big-picture strategy, program development

Local Computer Folder Structure

Each team member creates this folder on their own machine. It's personal scratch space — not a permanent home for anything.

📁 TWA-AI-Work/             ← Create on Desktop or Documents

Important: The local Working Drafts/ folder is temporary scratch space only. Once an output is ready for review, it goes to Google Drive → _AI Outputs → [Role] → Drafts. Local files are temporary. Drive is permanent.

Claude Project Setup (5 Steps)

1
Go to claude.ai → Sign in → Click "Projects" in the left sidebar
2
Click "New Project" → Name it: The Wide Awakening
3
Open MASTER - Claude Project Instructions.md from Google Drive → Copy all content → Paste into the Project Instructions field
4
Click "Add Content" (Knowledge) → Upload all three files from your local _Context/ folder: Brand Voice, Avatar Profile, Offer Library
5
Start your first session by stating your role and task. Example: "I'm the social media manager. We have an Awakened Conversations event in 3 weeks. I need a 2-week Instagram content calendar."
03 — Quick Reference

Rules & Troubleshooting

The 3 Golden Rules

1
Google Drive is the single source of truth — always
2
Nothing leaves Drafts without Jeannie's review
3
Update the MASTERs when anything changes — not in your head, in the file

When Something Goes Wrong

Output doesn't sound like Jennifer? → Update the Brand Voice doc
Wrong offer details in content? → Update the Offer Library
Team confused about where to save? → Re-run the naming convention doc
Instructions feel outdated? → Quarterly review is your fix
